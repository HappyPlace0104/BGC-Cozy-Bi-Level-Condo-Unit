Important

House Rules

👥 Max 8 Guests 🚭 No Smoking Inside 🪪 Registered Guests Only 💖 Good Vibes Only

🚭 No Smoking Cigarettes Inside the Unit

Smoking cigarettes or tobacco inside the unit is strictly prohibited. The strong odor affects the space for future guests and is difficult to remove. A ₱3,000 penalty will be applied for smoking cigarette violations.

👥 Maximum Occupancy

This unit accommodates up to 8 guests. Bedding will be prepared according to the number of declared guests, so please make sure your guest count is accurate at the time of booking.

🪪 Registered Guests Only

Only registered guests are allowed to stay overnight. Please make sure everyone staying is declared and checked in at the building lobby.

🌙 Quiet Hours: 10PM – 8AM

Please be mindful of noise levels during quiet hours out of respect for neighbors.

🏠 Care for the Space

The unit is carefully prepared before each check-in — clean, complete, and damage-free. Any pre-existing concerns are noted. Please treat the space with care and respect.

🎒 Lost Items

The host is not responsible for any lost items or incidents during your stay. Please keep your valuables secure.

🗑️ Trash

Please bag your trash before checkout and leave it inside the unit — housekeeping handles disposal. If the waste bin is full, dispose of it in the Garbage Room — turn right when you exit the unit door, walk a few steps and you will see it on the left side.

📺 Smart TV Accounts

You're welcome to sign into your own Netflix or streaming accounts on the Smart TVs. Please log out before checkout.

⚠️ Violations

Any violation of house rules — including smoking inside, exceeding the declared guest count, or allowing unregistered guests to stay overnight — will be reported to Airbnb and will incur a penalty for those who do not comply.

💚 Good vibes only! This space was built with love — please treat it as your own. We're sure you'll feel right at home.
